Overview

Purpose

Pillar Game Design is a company that will build the best current video games. My company will have a focus on the best games, and the best possible user interaction. We will provide games for every console, from Mac, to Windows, to Xbox, we will have it all. We will sell all of our games on our site, as well as on other popular sites, such as amazon. Our goal is to have the best user interaction through a strong community, and playing across consoles.

Audience

My target audience is all gamers, old and new. We will have games that the older folks will love and remember, as well as the new games for those kids just stepping into the amazing world of gaming. We will sell our own games as well as games that are made by other companies. We will have games for all platforms and consols, such as PC, Xbox, PS4/PS5, and even Mac. This way no matter which console you prefer, you can still join in on the fun.

Content

Home page

General Idea:
The general idea is to allow all people and ages the ability to enjoy gaming as much as we enjoy creating them. Using our site will be very simple, and you will be able to navigate the site with ease. We want all gamers to be able to buy games on our site, as well as buy consoles. These consoles can be used as Christmas gifts or just to get back into gaming. We want all to experience the joy of gaming.

Images for the Home page

Buying

Buying Games:
People will be able to buy games for any console and there will be a delivery option for console disk games, though most games will be digital. You will be able to find the games easy and see how many are in stock. We will also sell games exclusive to our site made by the developers at pillar game design.

Buying Consoles:
People will be able to buy consoles with delivery. We will show every console, and how much stock they have left, It will also include preowned consoles sold at a cheaper price.
